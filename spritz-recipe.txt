Spritz is the regular Venetian aperitivo, which is tasted almost everywhere in the Veneto region in Northern Italy.

Ingredients:
 - 3 Parts of Italian Prosecco D.o.c. (Designation of Origin) wine
 - 2 Parts Aperol aperitif
 - 1 Part of Soda
 - An orange slice
 - 3  ice cubes

Directions:

 1. Get a cocktail glass, and chill it by quickly rotating the ice cubes inside it with a circular motion
 
 2. Put all the ingredients into the chilled glass and top with with the orange slice 
 
 3. Say "Salute" and drink ;)
